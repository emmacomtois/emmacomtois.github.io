QUESTION

what are my best motion moments?

My love of motion design began in the fall of 2017 in a course on the topic. It's made its way into nearly every project I've completed since. Creating hierarchy and meaning through motion has altered my approach to design. Below are a collection of my favorite moments (so far) in motion.
