A little more about me.

I was born and raised in Los Angeles before moving across the country to study graphic design and finance at Syracuse University. Yes, it’s cold. No, I don’t know why I did that to myself. This year, Graphic Design USA named me a 2018 Student to Watch.

Most recently, I worked at Amazon as a design intern on the Alexa Shopping team. I helped launch the marketing for the new experience of shopping Whole Foods with Alexa.

When I’m not hanging out at The Daily Orange, you can catch me working on my Fear Factor audition tape (it’s going great, thanks for asking) or assembling a charcuterie board.
