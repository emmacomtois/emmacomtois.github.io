so, you want to know more …
I was born and raised in Los Angeles before moving across the country to study graphic design and finance at Syracuse University. Yes, it was cold. No, I don’t know why I did that to myself. Last year, Graphic Design USA named me a 2018 Student to Watch.

This past summer, I worked at Amazon on the Alexa Shopping team as a design intern helping launch the marketing campaign for a new experience: shopping Whole Foods with Alexa. This spring, I'm finishing up my senior project, applying the voice design skills I learned to one of my passions, sustainable fashion. I'll be returning to Amazon this fall working to help improve voice interaction design.

In my free time, I love to draw, watercolor, eat bread and butter (the soundtrack to my life is indeed Chateau Lobby #4 by Father John Misty), work on my Fear Factor audition tape and assemble charcuterie boards. If any of this sparks your interest, let's chat.
