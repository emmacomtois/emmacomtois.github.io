midcentury modern

ROLE

Art direction
Copywriting
Illustration
Print design

QUESTION

how can we make a print product necessary to a digital experience?

A fascination with midcentury modern furniture began when I studied abroad in Denmark in the fall of 2018. As an homage to this love of mine, I started a speculative book design that would integrate augmented reality. I wanted to find a way to bring this print medium into the digital space, so during a 30 day challenge, I illustrated 15 examples of midcentury modern design and prototyped the AR experience.

CHALLENGE #1

bring out the bold simplicity of midcentury design

The hallmark of midcentury modern design is beautiful utilitarianism. The best examples are simple, with restrained elegance. The illustrations needed to express that, but vectors felt too clean. There's a level of interest present in midcentury modern design, so I used gouache brushes to create texture and liveliness to these illustrations.
