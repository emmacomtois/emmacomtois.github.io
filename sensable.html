QUESTION

how do we educate people about sustainable fashion with all levels of awareness and define a niche ?

Compared to 16 years ago (2002), the average person buys 60% more clothing, but keep it for half as long. As the sustainable fashion movement has migrated from the fringes to mainstream markets, customers are searching for reliable resources that hold the fast fashion industry accountable. It's time to start shopping sustainable. Scratch that, shop Sensable.

PROBLEM SPACE

formulating an informative and accessible experience for all users

One of the main criticisms of the sustainable fashion industry is its lack of inclusivity. A lot of fashion brands do not have diverse models in terms of size, race, age or ability. Sensable aims to catch a wider net and educate all people on how they can learn and contribute to a more sustainable fashion industry. This is approached by including a wide array of ways to interact with the app that's reliant on more than just one sense. The app has voice and sound, touch and visual interaction capabilities so every user, no matter their awareness level can interact with the experience.
