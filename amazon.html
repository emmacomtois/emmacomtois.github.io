SUMMER 2018

amazon, alexa shopping + whole foods

ROLE
Art direction
Copywriting
Visual design

QUESTION
How do we introduce customers to shopping Whole Foods with Alexa?

CHALLENGE
Ensure customer clarity on using voice to shop for a historically visually-based activity, grocery shopping

DEVISING A SCALABLE CONCEPT

Coming up with a creative system that feels like a consistent campaign, but also works on large gateway placements or itsy-bitsy, teeny-weeny navigation bar placements was crucial. Add in the complexity of the uncharted territory of voice design and four brand systems, and you got yourself a real challenge.

CREATING CUSTOMER RESOURCES

This experience spanned multiple brands including Alexa Shopping, Amazon.com, Prime Now and Whole Foods, so the campaign needed multiple places to send customers when they needed a more in-depth explanation of the experience of shopping Whole Foods with Alexa.

A VISUAL SOLUTION TO A UX PROBLEM

A few days before launch, the team realized there was a customer population who may not receive push notifications, a key part of the user checkout and purchase experience, if certain settings were turned on. I designed and wrote copy for an email to remedy this UX problem by modifying settings in a few simple steps.
