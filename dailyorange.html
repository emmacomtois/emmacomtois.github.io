MOTION FALL 2015–SPRING 2019

The Daily Orange

Snapchat approached The Daily Orange in the spring of 2017 about producing a weekly Publisher story for their Discover page. The D.O. was one of the four original college newspapers to launch the program. These stories aim to drive community engagement by creating ultra-localized content. I lead a team of designers and animators creating 12 Snap concepts every week. Along with writing and sourcing content, I designed and wrote the custom HTML and CSS for The D.O.'s story.
